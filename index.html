About Me

Hello! I'm Surendra Pant, an enthusiastic Electrical and Electronics Engineer based in Kathmandu, Nepal, with a passion for innovation, technology, and problem-solving. I specialize in communication systems, networking, and IT support, combining academic knowledge with hands-on experience to deliver practical and efficient tech solutions.

I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (Communication) from Kathmandu University, and a Diploma in Electrical Engineering from CTEVT. My work spans a variety of impactful projects including IoT-based agriculture systems, SCARA robotic arms, beam steering using sensor fusion, and Nepal's first High Altitude Balloon Project.

My professional journey includes providing technical support, configuring network and security systems, and contributing to research-driven development in IT and electronics. I've also led national-level STEM initiatives like Aavishkar '24, collaborated with academic and municipal partners, and trained over 200 students in hands-on robotics and electronics.

My core skills include:

  • Programming: Python, C, C++, MATLAB, HTML, CSS
  • Networking & IT Support: LAN/WAN design, troubleshooting, system setup
